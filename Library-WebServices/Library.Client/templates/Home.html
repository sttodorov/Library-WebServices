A library worth visiting

This is team "Melon"`s library system.

Because we love reading and we like books to be good arranged and organized, we have created this app.

Our library system gives us opportunity to see all althors and books we have, to filter and delete authors by their names and to search for books by their title or author or remove them if they are no longer in the library.

Furthermore here you can add and delete authors and books and find briefly information for each book and if it is avaliable in our library.
